Honest answers

The questions everyone asks.

Will it sound like a robot?

No. Modern voices are natural, and the agent says it is an assistant. Customers care about being helped in two seconds, not about who picked up.

What happens with hard questions?

The assistant passes the conversation to your team with a full summary. It never guesses on things it does not know.

What about our data?

It stays yours. We build private setups where your documents and customer data never train public models.

Which languages?

Ten, in the same assistant: English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Polish, Ukrainian, Turkish, Portuguese and Arabic. It detects the customer's language and answers in it — even if your business works in one.

We are not a tech company. Will we manage?

That is exactly who we build for. We install, connect and run everything; your team keeps working the way they know. You get results and one number to call.

Will it replace my people?

No. It takes the routine — the same ten questions, the missed calls, the paperwork — so your people do the work only people can do.

What does it cost?

We price on outcome, starting from the audit. Most assistants cost less per month than one part-time employee, and you pay for results, not software licences.

How fast can we start?

The audit takes 30 minutes. The first working assistant answers real customers within weeks — the four steps above are the whole path.
